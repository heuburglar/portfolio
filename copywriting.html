Eat Well to Feel Great

At Mile High Organics we believe that mealtime should leave you nourished and empowered. Our commitment to your health means that we carefully screen all of our products, and their ingredients, to ensure that they are free of dangerous pesticides and chemicals, as well as genetically modified organisms. If the source of any ingredient is in doubt, we rule it out. The right food doesn't harm you - it loves you back!

What's Hot 

New Products are live and it's local vendor week. We're so excited to now offer a wide-range of products from local favorite Rudi's Organic Bakery, and chai teas from our friends at Bhakti Chai.

Fantastic seasonal pricing on organic broccoli and cauliflower continues. We hope to have our hands on some vibrant organic purple cauliflower later in the week, be sure to open your "Order Modification" reminder email for the opportunity to pick some up and see other great coupons.

New Local Partnerships

Bhakti Chai

We are so excited to now offer Bhakti Chai teas. Not only is Bhakti Chai a fantastic Colorado company, but they are our neighbors, literally. Let us know how much you love having their teas delivered and we'll run down the stairs and tell them!

Rudi's Organic Bakery

Rudi's has been a favorite organic choice for Coloradoans since 1976. At long last we've put all of the pieces in place and are offering a wide-range of Rudi's organic products from Cinnamon Raisin Bagels to Whole Wheat English Muffins. Find them all in the new Organic Bakery category.

MHO makes Organic Easy

Mile High Organics delivers all of your organic essentials. Put the items you need most on recurring order and we'll bring them to your door. Skip the grocery store and spend more time doing the things you love!

It's all About Fruit

Sure it was just Memorial Day, but you can really tell it's summer just by the fruit selection this week. Try organic yellow peaches, cantaloupe, mini seedless watermelon and red grapes. Be sure to enjoy the fruits of summer while you can.

Speaking of summer, what goes better with a long hot day than an ice cold beverage? Break up the heat with our new selection of fruit juices, nectars, and sparkling spritzers. With flavors ranging from basic to exotic you're sure to find something to please everyone!

It's Good to Know Your Grocer

Even though we're online we love to meet our members in the flesh. We'll be at the People's Fair this weekend and the Old South Pearl Farmer's Market on Sundays all summer long. Tell your friends to visit us and they'll receive a free organic cotton Mile High Organics tote bag just for signing up.

We're Certified Organic

We just became America's first fully certified organic online grocer! This means that every department in our store is certified organic.

We worked hard towards this certification to show you how we're different from all other grocers. We are the first online grocer in the United States to pursue this voluntary certification because we place the utmost importance on transparency and traceability.

We do this for you and thank-you all for supporting your local organic grocer.
